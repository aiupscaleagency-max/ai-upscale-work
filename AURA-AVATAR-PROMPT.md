# AURA — Avatar-prompt (Google Labs)

> AURA = Mikes generativa AI, andra hjärna + HQ. En digitaliserad kvinna.
> Använd i: **Imagen** (stillbild) eller **Veo / Gemini Omni** (live-loop video).

---

## VARIANT A — Pixel/holografisk robot-kvinna (film-stil)

```
A photorealistic digital woman rendered as a holographic AI assistant, head and
shoulders, facing the camera. Her form is built from glowing cyan and electric-blue
light particles and fine pixel/voxel detail, like a sci-fi movie hologram (Iron Man
JARVIS aesthetic). Calm, intelligent, friendly expression. Subtle data streams and
HUD rings rotating softly behind her. Dark navy background (#020810). Soft cyan rim
lighting on her face. Elegant, futuristic, not cold — warm and approachable.
Ultra-detailed, cinematic, 4k. Centered composition, head slightly turned.
```

## VARIANT B — Halvt människa, halvt robot

```
A photorealistic portrait of a digital woman who is half human, half robot. The left
side of her face is natural, warm human skin; the right side reveals sleek brushed-metal
and glowing cyan circuitry seamlessly integrated, with soft light beneath translucent
panels. Intelligent, calm, confident expression. Head and shoulders, facing camera.
Dark navy studio background (#020810) with subtle blue HUD glow. Cinematic rim lighting,
ultra-detailed, 4k. Elegant and approachable — a trusted AI partner, not a machine.
```

---

## För LIVE-loop (Veo / Gemini Omni) — lägg till efter prompten:

```
Animate as a 6-second seamless loop: she breathes softly, blinks naturally, light
particles drift, HUD rings rotate slowly. Subtle idle motion — looks alive and ready
to speak. No camera movement. Loop-friendly start and end.
```

---

## Tekniska parametrar
| Inställning | Värde |
|---|---|
| Aspect ratio | **9:16** (för avatar-panel) eller **1:1** (cirkel-avatar) |
| Stil | Cinematic, photorealistic, soft cyan rim light |
| Bakgrund | Mörk navy `#020810` (matchar agent-os JARVIS-tema) |
| Verktyg bild | Imagen (AI Studio) eller Whisk |
| Verktyg live | Veo eller Gemini Omni (video-loop) |

## Nästa steg för ÄKTA live-avatar (Fas 3)
1. Generera AURA:s utseende (prompten ovan) → bild eller loop
2. Lägg den som AURA:s ansikte i agent-os Agent Hub
3. Koppla läppsynk-lager (driver munrörelser från Gemini Live-ljud) → äkta talande avatar

Tips: börja med **Variant A som bild i 1:1** → snyggast i ögat/avatar-panelen direkt.
