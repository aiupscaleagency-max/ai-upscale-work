# n8n Workflows

Lägg alla exporterade n8n workflows (JSON-format) här.

## Namnkonvention
`[KATEGORI]_[NAMN]_v[VERSION].json`

Exempel:
- `LINKEDIN_OutreachSequence_v1.json`
- `LEAD_Scoring_v2.json`
- `ONBOARDING_NewClient_v1.json`

## Kategorier
- `LINKEDIN` — LinkedIn outreach & automation
- `LEAD` — Lead scoring & enrichment
- `ONBOARDING` — Klient onboarding
- `INTERNAL` — Interna processer & rapporter
- `AI` — AI-agent workflows (Claude/GPT)
